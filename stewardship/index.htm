Stewardship of Digital Resources

This page describes the assets associated with the CDASH project and the concerns regarding their long-term preservation. Assets involve custom code, hundreds of thousands of scanned historical documents, and descriptive information about each of those documents. These assets have been developed at great expense over many years. We hope that these assets will remain intact indefinitely as files along with their catalog and organization that makes them discoverable. To that end, it is useful to describe the nature of the assets and our expectations regarding routine storage, as well as the predictable ways that the assets and their organization may become lost, corrupt, recovered or unrecoverable.

Responsibility for the on-going stewardship of the collection rests with individual municipal employees. This document identifies the roles of the key positions involved.
